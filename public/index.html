About The Paddock

BUILT FOR PEOPLE WHO WRENCH

The Paddock is a private, members-only car storage and DIY workshop in San Francisco. We built it because this city has no shortage of car enthusiasts and an almost total shortage of space to store and work on them.

We've kept the membership deliberately small — 17 vehicles maximum — so the space never feels crowded and the community stays tight-knit. Everyone here knows their cars and respects everyone else's.

Whether you're maintaining a weekend track car, restoring a classic, or just need somewhere secure to store a vehicle you can't keep at home — The Paddock is where you belong.
